ASGARD is an open world survival game where the player is left alone to explore an open medieval world by themselves.

In this small game demo i have created a small open map that the player can traverse, they player is given a quest to find lost gems whilst surviving and escaping from hostile encounters
I was inspired to create the game with this theme by other games such as elden ring and dark souls as they both feature the same smiliar open world style.
The game demo includes an old abandoned village laid out across from a small scenic lake, the player must explore around the map and fight off wolves and hostiles aswell as hunt deer to survive.

The game will be available soon and will be FREE to play.
