NEWS AND IMPORTANT INFO
ASGARD will be shown at a game showcase on the 16th of June. You will be able to see and get hands on access to the game. It will just be a game demo, so it will just be a small area of the full map that is available to play and explore.

This will be an opportunity for more people to test and get their hands on the game, to find and fix any bugs that may come up when being played.


In the game demo, you will be exploring a small vilage and will be tasked with finding the lost gems that are scattered around the map, all the while surviving wolf attacks. There will be more updates to come so make sure to follow ASGARD on social media.

The showcase will be held at Bournemouth and Poole college.
